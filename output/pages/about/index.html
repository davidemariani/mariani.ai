About

Hi!

I am Davide, a London based Data Scientist currently working at Tradeteq, a Fintech operating in Trade Finance.
Here I spend time on graphs, machine learning and deep learning for predictive modelling.

NLP and graph neural networks are among my main interests, and I am constantly trying to get better at them with multiple incomplete side projects and not very successful Kaggle competitions.

I hold an MSc in Data Science from Birkbeck, University of London, and a long cycle degree (BEng + MSc) in Engineering for Buildings and Architecture from University of Pisa.

Before moving to AI, I worked as a computational design specialist across Europe and Asia for several architectural firms like Grimshaw Architects, Woods Bagot and MAD Architects.
